Jingnan Chen

I am a Senior Lecturer in Economics at the University of Exeter Business School. My research uses experimental methods to study how institutions, information, and incentives shape economic behaviour. My work focuses on three interconnected themes: cooperation and collective decision-making, gender and participation in leadership and competition, and decision-making under risk and uncertainty.
